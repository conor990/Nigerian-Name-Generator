The App

This application is an ongoing project, currently being improved and updated. This application was created primarily for the Nigerian diaspora to connect with their heritage using names. The name of a child holds incredible importance in many Nigerian cultures, therefore I intend to use this app to document as many Nigerian names as possiple and eventually include their meanings next to them.
